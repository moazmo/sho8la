Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" in the top right, enter your details, and choose whether you want to hire (Client) or work (Freelancer).

How does payment work?

Clients fund projects via InstaPay or Vodafone Cash. Money is held in escrow and released to freelancers upon approval.

How do I verify my student ID?

Go to your Profile, click "Verify Now", upload your university ID, and wait for admin approval (usually within 24 hours).

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

You can request revisions or contact support. Payment is only released when you approve the work.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Go to Wallet, click "Withdraw", enter the amount, and select your payment method. Funds arrive within 1-3 business days.

Is there a fee for using Sho8la?

Sho8la charges a 10% service fee on completed projects, split between client and freelancer.
