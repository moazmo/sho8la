Help & Support

How do I post a job?

Click "Post Job" in the navigation menu, fill in the details, and submit. Your job will be visible to all students.

How does payment work?

Payments are held in escrow. The client releases payment after confirming the work is complete.

Why do I need to verify my ID?

Verification ensures all users are real students, building trust in the community.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@sho8la.com or use the help section in your profile.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept InstaPay, Vodafone Cash, and other local Egyptian payment methods.

Can I change my role after signup?

Yes, you can switch roles from your profile settings at any time.
